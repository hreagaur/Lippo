Unlock Your Inner Beauty with Blush Blossom

Experience the subtle elegance and youthful charm of Blush Blossom, our signature lipstick shade that's as delightful as it is versatile. Like a whisper of springtime on your lips, Blush Blossom invites you to embrace your natural beauty and radiate confidence with every swipe.

At Lippo-Stick, we're committed to transparency and ethical practices. Blush Blossom is crafted with carefully selected ingredients, ensuring that each swipe is not only beautiful but also nourishing for your lips. Our vegan formula excludes any animal-derived ingredients, making Blush Blossom a cruelty-free choice you can feel good about.

Ingredients & Vegan Certification

Blush Blossom is crafted with a vegan formula, free from animal-derived ingredients and cruelty-free. Our key ingredients include:

Avocado Oil: Rich in antioxidants, nourishes lips deeply.
Coconut Oil: Locks in moisture, keeping lips soft.
Aloe Vera Extract: Soothes and hydrates for healthy lips
Cocoa Butter: Provides intense hydration, leaving lips feeling silky.
Fish Oil: Softens and smoothens lips with its emollient properties
Almond Oil: Softens and smoothens lips with its emollient properties
